Orlando Theme Parks

Visitors both young and young at heart can immerse themselves in the world's top theme parks in Orlando. From the classic Magic Kingdom at Walt Disney World Resort to The Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Orlando Resort, to Islands of Adventure, there's a world for everyone to escape into! Experience a wonderland of ocean animals at SeaWorld Orlando. Discover fun for the whole family at LEGOLAND Florida, one of Orlando's new parks.

Florida Ghost Tours

While Halloween brings to mind ghosts and goblins, did you know that many ghostly tours are available throughout the year? Florida offers a collection of tours and excursions guaranteed to scare up the need to plan a day trip or weekend getaway. As entertaining as they are informative, ghost tours explore paranormal phenomenon that may have you believing... or not. Offered everywhere from Amelia Island, to St. Augustine, and even Daytona Beach.

South Florida Water Sports

Whether you are a top notch wakeboarder or water skier – or just a first timer – these experienced experts will get you out on the water having fun! With a group of up to eight people, you can all enjoy the water sports offered during your charter. Water sports include waterskiing, wakeboarding, water surfing and tubing. They also offer boat tours captained by a fully licensed captain and professional instruction for your safety and enjoyment.

The Daytona 500 Experience

Every racecar driver wants to win the Daytona 500 and now you can feel what it’s like with Richard Petty Driving Experience at Daytona International Speedway. The views of “The World’s Most Famous Beach” are amazing, but nothing beats the view from driving or riding inside a 600HP NASCAR race car on the 31-degree high banks of the historic 2.5-mile Daytona International Speedway and racing thrills while friends and family cheer you on.
